# Research Sources — What to Extract

Run these in parallel during Step 3 of the workflow.

---

## LinkedIn (Public Profile)

**Search:** `"[Full Name]" "[Company]" site:linkedin.com/in`
**Fetch:** The profile URL directly

**Extract:**
- Current title and company
- Prior roles (last 3–5 positions), tenure at each
- Education (school, degree, graduation year)
- Recent posts or activity (last 30–60 days)
- Skills and endorsements
- Any mutual connections signals

---

## ZoomInfo (Public Pages)

**Search:** `"[Full Name]" "[Company]" site:zoominfo.com`

**Extract:**
- Title confirmation
- Department
- Company: revenue range, employee count, HQ location, industry
- Tech stack signals (what software the company uses)
- Org chart position signals

---

## RocketReach (Public)

**Search:** `"[Full Name]" "[Company]" site:rocketreach.co`

**Extract:**
- Role confirmation
- Company context
- Seniority / department

---

## Company Website

**Fetch:** `[company].com/about`, `[company].com/leadership`, `[company].com/press`

**Extract:**
- Company mission, products, customers
- Leadership team (who reports to who)
- Press releases from last 12 months
- Any mention of Salesforce, digital transformation, AI

---

## Web News

**Search:** `"[Company]" news 2025 2026`
**Search:** `"[Company]" funding OR acquisition OR "new CEO" OR "digital transformation" 2025 2026`

**Extract:**
- Funding rounds, valuations
- M&A activity
- Leadership changes
- Product launches
- Analyst coverage (Gartner, Forrester)

---

## Salesforce Ecosystem

**Search:** `"[Company]" Salesforce implementation OR "Service Cloud" OR "Marketing Cloud" OR "Data Cloud"`
**Search:** `"[Company]" site:appexchange.salesforce.com`
**Search:** `"[Company]" Dreamforce OR "Salesforce World Tour" speaker`

**Extract:**
- Which Salesforce clouds in use or under evaluation
- Known SI/implementation partners
- AppExchange presence
- Event participation / speaking history

---

## Outlook Email History

**Tool:** `outlook_email_search`
**Query:** `[Company] OR [Contact Name]`

**Extract:**
- Prior email threads with this contact or company
- Event invitations (joint Salesforce/EPAM/customer events)
- Prior meeting outcomes
- EPAM colleagues who have prior relationship with this account

---

## Crunchbase (Startups / Growth Companies)

**Search:** `"[Company]" site:crunchbase.com`
**Fetch:** The Crunchbase page

**Extract:**
- Funding history (Series A/B/C, total raised, last round date)
- Key investors
- Headcount growth
- Founded year, founders

---

## SEC EDGAR (Public Companies)

**Search:** `"[Company]" 10-K OR "annual report" site:sec.gov`

**Extract:**
- Revenue, growth rate
- Key business risks
- Technology / digital investment mentions
- Competitive positioning

---

## Glassdoor / LinkedIn Company Page

**Search:** `"[Company]" site:glassdoor.com`

**Extract:**
- Company culture signals
- Recent reviews mentioning technology / transformation
- Headcount trends (growing or contracting)

---

## What NOT to Do

- Do not fabricate details that cannot be confirmed from research
- Do not assume Salesforce product usage without a source
- Do not include personal information beyond professional public profiles
- Do not use information from paywalled sources (ZoomInfo full profiles, LinkedIn Sales Navigator) — public tiers only
